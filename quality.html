Quality

We are committed to achieving the highest quality in the products and services we offer: from suppliers, to production, to final product, to distribution.

In early 2021, we achieved FSSC22000 (Food Safety System Certification Scheme 22000) certification, a food safety reference standard recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI).

Artisanity

We are convinced that putting human beings and their manual skills at the centre of the production process is an added value. Behind every product there is a person who, with creativity, energy and intelligence, guarantees the excellence of the work. Personal contact and transparency are reflected in the relationship with our stakeholders. Our modern infrastructure, combined with our craftsmanship approach, ensures the right balance between quality and efficiency even on large production volumes.

Respect For Nature & Territory

Our gourmet spirit, infinite creativity and the gastronomic techniques we use allow us to create innovative and tasty recipes, without adding food additives, colourings or preservatives. The passion of our master pasta makers is the basic ingredient in every product we make.

Passion For Gastronomy

Security & Control

Our mission is to create, develop and commercialize fresh and tasty food products, based on three fundamental pillars of our business ethics: craftsmanship, respect for nature and the territory, and passion for gastronomy. Food safety and control processes are the foundation on which our three values rest.
